How to Play
NYX : SONAR
◉
Ping to See
Tap or click to send a sonar pulse. The echo reveals obstacles hidden in the dark. Without pinging — you're blind.
↑
Tap to Jump
Every tap makes your signal orb jump. Gravity pulls it down. Time your taps to fly through the gap in each wall.
🎯
Clear 10 Walls
Pass through 10 wall gaps to complete a level. Each level adds new dangers. 10 levels total.
