Privacy Policy for NYX: SONAR

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

NYX: SONAR is a free arcade game developed by AR Game Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our game. By playing NYX: SONAR, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the game.

2. Information We Collect

NYX: SONAR does not directly collect or store personal information (such as your name, address, or email) on our own servers. The game stores gameplay data such as scores, unlocks, and settings locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect Advertising IDs and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can view Google's Privacy Policy here.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used to understand usage patterns and improve game performance. This data is collected in an anonymized form.

4. How We Use Information

The information collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within NYX: SONAR.
  • Measure ad performance and improve the overall user experience.
  • Diagnose technical issues, crashes, and fix bugs.

5. Local Data Storage

NYX: SONAR saves game progress, high scores, unlocked items, and settings locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app cache.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

NYX: SONAR is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data, please contact us immediately at the email listed below for prompt deletion.

7. User Consent (GDPR/CCPA)

For users in the EU/EEA and UK, we implement the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage consent for personalized advertising. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time through the settings within NYX: SONAR.

8. Data Retention & Security

AR Game Studio does not store personal data on its own servers. Data collected by our third-party partners is retained according to their respective industry-standard policies. While we use commercially acceptable means to protect information, please be aware that no method of mobile transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Information collected by third-party services (like Google AdMob) may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, or country. By using NYX: SONAR, you consent to these transfers.
